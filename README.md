KhedmaNow is a **service-on-demand platform** designed to digitize the interaction between customers and service companies. 
The platform allows users to browse service categories, submit detailed service requests, and connects them with trusted companies who can manage orders and publish dynamic offers.

---

## üöÄ Features

### 1- Location-based Services
- Detects user location to connect them with companies covering their specific zone (e.g., Cairo, Giza).
- Allows pinning exact addresses on the map for accurate service delivery.
- **Why it matters:** ensures logistical feasibility and reduces "out of coverage" cancellations.

---

### 2- Distinct User Roles
- **Customers:** Browse categories ‚Üí Fill technical forms ‚Üí Request Service ‚Üí Track Status.
- **Service Companies:** Manage service portfolio, view incoming request queues, and publish hot offers.
- **Why it matters:** tailored dashboards that streamline the workflow for both demand and supply sides.

---

### 3- Smart Request System (Dynamic Forms)
- Context-aware forms that change based on the service (e.g., asking for "AC Horsepower" vs. "Number of Rooms").
- Allows clients to upload photos/media of the issue during the request.
- **Why it matters:** eliminates ambiguity and ensures the company arrives prepared with the right tools.

---

### 4- Company Dashboard & Offers
- **Request Management:** A Kanban-style view for companies to Accept, Reject, or Schedule incoming orders.
- **Dynamic Offers Module:** Companies can create and publish time-limited promotions (e.g., "Summer AC Checkup - 20% Off").
- **Why it matters:** empowers companies to actively drive sales and manage operations efficiently.

---

### 5- Real-time Order Tracking
- **Status Lifecycle:** Request Received ‚Üí Confirmed ‚Üí Technician Assigned ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Completed.
- Clients can view the live status of their order from their dashboard.
- **Why it matters:** reduces anxiety and the need for follow-up phone calls.

---

### 6- Payment Options
- **Cash on Delivery** as the primary method to ensure trust.
- Invoicing system where companies generate a digital receipt upon completion.
- **Why it matters:** aligns with local market preferences and simplifies adoption.

---

### 7- Verified Ratings & Reviews
- Available only after a service is marked as **Completed**.
- Service-specific ratings (rating the "Plumbing Service" specifically, not just the company generally).
- **Why it matters:** ensures high quality standards and helps good companies stand out.

---

### 8- Admin Dashboard
- **Platform Health:** Monitor total requests, active companies, and user growth.
- **Verification:** Approve or suspend service companies and verify business documents.
- **Service Management:** Define main categories and required data fields.
- **Why it matters:** ensures the safety, quality, and scalability of the marketplace.
